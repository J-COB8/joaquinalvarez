What we built

3 new product features
we are proposing to implement

Each feature is a concrete UI change backed by a behavioral economics principle. Here is exactly what changes in the product.

1
New UI · Replaces add-on list

Gastronomic Canopy

We replace the flat add-on list with a visual plate canvas.

Items slide onto an illustrated plate as the user taps them. “Frites” becomes Salt-Crusted Russet Wedges with Smoked Thyme Aioli — one cohesive price, zero micro-friction.

+28%
lift in companion item orders
Why it works
Attribute framing eliminates the “mental toll booth” of multiple small purchases — the customer perceives one premium experience, not three line items.
2
New UI · Appears at checkout

Morning-After Bridge

We offer tomorrow’s breakfast inside today’s order.

A vacuum-sealed croissant + nitro cold brew kit, temperature-sleeved, delivered now. Framed as “skip tomorrow’s delivery fee” — not as extra spending.

+$4.50
added to a $10 order (+45%)
Why it works
Present bias: the satiation barrier only blocks spending on now. Tomorrow’s food feels like a future saving, not a current cost.
3
New UI · Appears after cart is complete

The Final Accord

A one-tap micro-dessert carousel at the very end of checkout.

Molten dark truffle, panna cotta, botanical palate cleanser — $2 each. Only appears once the cart is done. Framed as a reward, not an upsell.

+$2–3
added to 40% of all orders
Why it works
Kahneman’s Peak-End Rule: memory judges an experience by its last moment. A $2 sweet at the end reframes the entire meal as premium — and drives repeat orders.

Implementation

Four-phase rollout

Phase 1
Thermal Sleever Trial
Launch with 50 top-performing restaurant partners. Pilot Morning-After packaging. Measure acceptance rates and temperature integrity.
Phase 2
Interactive Plating UI
Replace the flat add-on list with the Gastronomic Canopy across iOS and Android. A/B test against control. Measure AOV delta and cart abandonment.
Phase 3
Tomorrow Loop API
Integrate the Morning-After Bridge with partner cafes and bakeries. Dynamic pricing engine. Loyalty integration for repeat ritual customers.
Phase 4
Global Deployment
Scale across all markets with localized Gastronomic Canopy pairings, regional Final Accord desserts, and DoorDash/Deliveroo competitive blocking.
